Salesforce Data Audit

CRM Data Quality and Marketing Database Analysis

Project Overview

This project focuses on auditing and improving the quality of marketing data stored in Salesforce. The objective was to identify duplicate records, missing fields, and inconsistencies affecting campaign reporting accuracy.

Tools Used

  • Salesforce
  • Excel
  • Data Cleaning Techniques
  • CRM Data Analysis

Key Insights

  • Over 12% of leads were identified as duplicates, affecting marketing attribution and campaign performance reporting.
  • Incomplete contact records reduced segmentation accuracy for targeted campaigns.
  • Implementing data validation rules improved data consistency and reduced manual data correction work.
  • Improved CRM data quality enabled more reliable campaign performance reporting.
