Trading System Performance Analytics

Performance Analysis of Automated Trading Strategies

Project Overview

This project analyzes the performance of automated trading systems by tracking key metrics such as profitability, trade frequency, win rate, and drawdown. The analysis helps evaluate system stability and risk exposure.

Tools Used

  • Excel
  • Data Visualization
  • Performance Analytics
  • FXBlue

Key Insights

  • Trading performance showed consistent monthly profitability with moderate drawdown levels.
  • Win rate analysis indicated that risk management settings had a stronger impact on profitability than trade frequency.
  • Trade clustering during high volatility periods increased overall exposure risk.
  • Adjusting lot sizing and trade timing improved risk-adjusted returns.
