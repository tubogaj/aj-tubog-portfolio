About Me

I am a Marketing Operations and Data Analyst with experience in marketing analytics, CRM data management and performance reporting. I specialize in transforming complex data into actionable insights that support business decisions and marketing strategy.

My work includes building analytics dashboards, auditing CRM data quality, and developing internal tools that help teams monitor performance and improve operational efficiency. I have worked closely with marketing teams and account managers to analyze campaign performance, evaluate client account growth, and optimize reporting workflows.

As a freelancer, I focus on helping businesses streamline their operations through data analysis, marketing reporting, CRM optimization, and internal system development. I also support teams with virtual assistance, documentation systems, and website development to improve productivity and organization.

I am passionate about using data, automation, and modern tools to simplify processes, improve visibility into business performance, and help organizations scale more efficiently.
